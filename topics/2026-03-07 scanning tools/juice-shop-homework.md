## Learning about Juice Shop

This week, please use the [OWASP Juice Shop Companion Guide](https://pwning.owasp-juice.shop/companion-guide/latest/index.html) and see if you can solve some of the Juice Shop challenges. Before you start, read the **Preface** section, and the sections called *Hacking exercise rules* and *Walking the "happy path"* from **Part I - Hacking preparations**. These sections will help you understand the expectations of the people who maintain the app and get you familiar with the Juice Shop site from a customer's point of view.

After that, it's time to dig in!

## Hacking Juice Shop

In **Part II - Challenge hunting**, they list the challenges and their difficulties (just like the Juice Shop application does on its scoreboard page). However, they also have solutions that walk you through how to solve each problem.

What you should do is create a login on your Juice Shop instance, find the scoreboard, and then begin by trying to do some of the easiest challenges first (the easiest ones are marked with a single star). After giving a problem some thought, don't feel bad about using the solutions that the Guide has if you are stumped. You are just beginning to learn about these things, and it's OK if you need help, as long as you try to learn from what the solution has you do.
